About KUJAS

KolaDaisi University Journal of Applied Sciences (KUJAS) is a peer-reviewed academic journal established by the Faculty of Applied Sciences, KolaDaisi University, Ibadan, Nigeria. Launched in 2023, KUJAS is devoted to publishing innovative research and scholarly reviews that advance knowledge in the pure and applied sciences, including technology, engineering, pharmaceutical sciences, and medicine.

Our Vision

To be a leading journal of applied scientific research in Nigeria and across Africa — driving innovation, promoting integrity in research, and supporting academic excellence.

Scope

KUJAS welcomes contributions in a broad range of scientific and technological fields, including but not limited to:

  • Biological and Chemical Sciences
  • Mathematical and Computing Sciences
  • Engineering and Environmental Technology
  • Health, Pharmaceutical, and Medical Sciences
  • Multidisciplinary Applied Research

We accept original research articles, short communications, and concise review articles with interdisciplinary relevance.

Editorial Integrity

All submissions undergo a double-blind peer review process conducted by experts in the field. Our editorial board comprises distinguished academics from leading institutions in Nigeria and internationally, ensuring rigorous scholarly standards and ethical oversight.

Publishing Policy

KUJAS is published twice a year and upholds ethical publishing practices, discouraging plagiarism, predatory submissions, and publication misconduct. Authors are required to submit manuscripts in accordance with our Author Guidelines and to declare potential conflicts of interest.

ISSN and Indexing

  • Print ISSN: 2955–0734
  • Electronic ISSN: 2955–0742

Publisher

KUJAS is published by the Faculty of Applied Sciences, KolaDaisi University, a private institution committed to academic excellence and research development.
